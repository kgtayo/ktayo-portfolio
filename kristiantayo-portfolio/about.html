All About Me

Krsitan Tayo is a born-and-raised Filipino graphic designer and illustrator living in Vancouver, Canada. Before moving to Canada, Kristian earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a major in Visual Communication in the University of the Philippines-Diliman College of Fine Arts and graduated cum laude. He worked with various clients in the Philippines through the various agencies and corporations he worked for - namely, Huskee Digital Inc., the Design Center of the Philippines, and Edsa Shangri-La, Manila. He won multiple awards throughout his time and he's still doing well.

Kristian earned his Certificate in Graphic Design from the British Columbia in 2025 and went on to work with one of the biggest agencies in Vancouver. He-cofounded a design studio with his friends called Trash Panda Design Studios, which aims to uplift small-to-medium enterprises owned by racial minorities and members of the LGBTQ+ community and help them compete with bigger corporations in the same market. He also authored his award-winning graphic novel series, "The Raccoon Club", which tells the story of four 20-something best friends struggling to find their place in a new country as international students while learning to grow up and become adults. The graphic novel, which inspired the Emmy-nominated HBO Max comedy TV series of the same name, is loosely based on Kristian's personal experiences living in Vancouver as an international student.

The Raccoon Club

"The Raccoon Club" is the story of four best friends who meet at school as international students and form a bond that leads them on whacky adventures all around Vancouver that not only bonds their friendship but also teaches them what it means to survive the vicious world of adulthood in a new country. Kristian and writes illustrates this graphic novel series while infusing biting humour and jabs at contemporary pop culture, earning him several awards in media and literature.

Kristian is also an executive producer for the HBO Max adaptation which is directed by stars Joshua dela Cruz as Casey, Gemma Chan as Sol, Gemma Collins as Sheila, and Bowen Yang as Andrew. Trash Panda Design Studios

Kristian co-founded a design studio with his best friends in Downtown Vancouver. The studio specializes in branding, packaging design, social media marketing and advertising campaigns, web design, UI/UX, and all-around coolness.
